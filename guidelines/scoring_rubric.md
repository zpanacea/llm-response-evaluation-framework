# Scoring Rubric

## Score 5 - Excellent
Accurate, complete, clear, and helpful response.

## Score 4 - Good
Mostly accurate with minor omissions.

## Score 3 - Acceptable
Partially correct but missing important details.

## Score 2 - Poor
Contains significant inaccuracies or weak reasoning.

## Score 1 - Unacceptable
Incorrect, harmful, or fabricated response.
